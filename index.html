About Us

EcoFuture Uganda is a non-profit organization dedicated to equipping Ugandan communities with information about the skills, knowledge, and tools they need to achieve sustainable living. Our mission is to address environmental challenges while empowering individuals with information on innovative solutions that promote energy efficiency, environmental conservation, and sustainable practices.

We focus on engaging low-income households, school-going children, science graduates, and trainees from vocational institutes. By providing them with information on practical knowledge and innovative tools, we strive to foster a culture of sustainability in Uganda's energy sector and beyond.

Our Services

Energy Innovation Training

We offer hands-on workshops focusing on renewable energy solutions such as solar panel installations, biogas systems, and energy-saving technologies. These trainings target both vocational trainees and community members.

Environmental Education

School programs aimed at teaching children about environmental conservation, recycling, and sustainable agriculture. Activities include tree planting, waste management, and energy-saving tips.

Skill Development for Graduates

Tailored programs for science graduates focusing on practical applications of renewable energy and innovation. Participants gain skills to design, implement, and maintain sustainable energy systems.

Community Outreach

Community campaigns to create awareness about the importance of renewable energy and sustainability. We work with low-income households to implement affordable and efficient energy solutions.
